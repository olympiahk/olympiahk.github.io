There are different styles of music

There is classical music, jazz music, pop music and many others.

Some instruments can play more than one type of music

For example, the piano can be used in classical music and in pop music.

This is an introduction to the four instrument families

A small description of each instrument family

A first instrument family

The woodwind instruments are clarinets, flutes, saxophone, trombone and many other instrument.

A second instrument family

Some of the instruments in the brass family are cornet, baritone and tuba.

A Third instrument family

There is a string family. The cello and the guitar belong to this family.

A fourth instrument family

Another family is the percussion family which contains drums, xylophones, cymbals and many other instrument.
